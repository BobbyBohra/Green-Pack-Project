Document: Plastic Waste Management Rules 2016 - Summary
Source: Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, India

Plastic Categories and Limits:
1. Rigid Plastic: Includes bottles, containers, crates. Monthly declaration required.
2. Flexible Plastic: Includes bags, films, pouches. Monthly declaration required.
3. Multilayer Plastic: Includes sachets, packaging with multiple layers. Monthly declaration required.

Declaration Tolerance:
- A variance of up to 5% between declared quantity and actual procurement is acceptable.
- Variance above 5% must be explained in writing within 7 days.
- Repeated variance above 10% may lead to show cause notice.

Reconciliation Process:
- Producers must reconcile declared quantities with ERP/procurement records monthly.
- Discrepancies must be flagged and corrected in next month's declaration.
- Third party audit required annually for producers above 1000 tonnes per year.
